ABOUT KRISHNA VATIKA
____


Krishna Vatika is a luxury designer label synonymous with a contemporary bespoke experience in the Indian fashion arena. Each garment at our store is a symphony of minimalistic sensibility, fluid tailoring and sophisticated geometric detailing.


We cater to an elite clientele - sartorially inclined individuals who know and understand the value of the bespoke process including a range of influencer personalities from Bollywood and the global sports industry.
