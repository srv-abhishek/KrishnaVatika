FAQs





WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY PURCHASED GARMENT?
A Sarah & Sandeep garment takes a minimum of 25 days to be crafted. Once it has been completed, it is shipped almost immediately. The shipping time depends on your location around the globe.
DO I NEED AN ACCOUNT TO PLACE AN ORDER?
An order can be placed with a registered account or you could choose to checkout as guest. We recommend registering so the styles can be curated as per your sensibilities.
HOW DO I REGISTER FOR AN ACCOUNT?
To register, click on the Sign Up icon placed on the top right corner of the screen and proceed to create your Sarah & Sandeep account.
I HAVE FORGOTTEN MY PASSWORD. WHAT DO I DO?
Follow the ‘Forgot your password?’ link on our Sign In page and enter your email address. You’ll then receive an email to reset your password.
WHERE CAN I FIND SIZE & FIT ADVICE?
Click on ‘Size Chart’ on the item’s page to view our conversion chart. We also provide fit information and model measurements for each style under the ‘Size & Fit’ tab.
WHICH CURRENCIES CAN I SHOP IN?
The currency that you shop with is determined by your shipping destination and will be displayed at checkout before completing your purchase. If your local currency is unavailable, your order will be charged in INR for a competitive exchange rate.
WILL MY PERSONAL DETAILS STAY SAFE?
We keep your personal data private and confidential and only give it out with your consent or if legally permitted to. For further details, please see our privacy policy under terms and conditions.
WHAT IS BESPOKE CLOTHING?
Traditionally, Bespoke clothing is cut from a pattern drafted and designed from scratch specifically for the customer. This service differs significantly from the made to measure process wherein standard sized garments are merely altered to suit client specifications.
DO I NEED TO MAKE AN APPOINTMENT?
A prior appointment is required for our bespoke services, You can book an appointment on our contact us Page. Alternatively, you can do an online consultation with one of our bespoke specialists. Our timings are 12:00 PM to 7:30 PM.
